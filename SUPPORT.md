## Support Process

1. **Initial Contact**: Choose the appropriate support channel
2. **Information Gathering**: Provide relevant details about your issue
3. **Triage**: We'll assess and route your request
4. **Resolution**: We'll work to resolve your issue
5. **Follow-up**: We'll ensure the solution meets your needs

## What to Include

### Bug Reports
- Steps to reproduce
- Expected vs actual behavior
- Environment details
- Error messages or logs
- Screenshots if applicable

### Feature Requests
- Use case description
- Business impact
- Proposed solution
- Alternative approaches considered

### Technical Issues
- System configuration
- Recent changes
- Error logs
- Network information
